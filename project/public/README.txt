This file include a brief desctiption about the added features in phase number 3.

** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** **

For the whole site:

Dark mode theme is now available, it can be achieved by pressing on the sun icon at the top of the header.

Three pages are added, login page, sign-up for doctors and sign-up for patients.

** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** **

Home page:

Nav bar can will be minimized bellow 768px and reached using a menu buttom appearing at the top right of the header.

A new form is added in order to send contact the admins of the site, it is acheived by pressing the Contact Us buttom.

** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** **

Sign-in page:

The page requests a name, either "doctor" or "patient" in order to reach the desired pages.

It can also lead new users to sign-up pages.

** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** **

Sign-up page:

This page is still for future work, but for now, sign-up button will lead to the login page.
